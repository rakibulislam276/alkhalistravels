Our Story

Two Decades of Trusted Travel

Founded in 2005 in the heart of Mumbai, Al Khalis Tours and Travels began with a singular mission: to provide honest, affordable and spiritually guided travel services to the Muslim community and beyond.

What started as a small Hajj and Umrah facilitation office in Saki Naka has grown into a full-service travel agency with two offices, serving over 15,000 happy travellers across India.

Our team brings together decades of expertise in international travel, religious tourism and customer service — backed by IATA certification, TAAI membership and Ministry of Hajj approval.
