Al Khalis Tours and Travels (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. Because we arrange flights, visas, hotels, Hajj & Umrah pilgrimages and foreign exchange, we necessarily handle personal and travel documents on your behalf. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We handle personal data in accordance with applicable Indian law, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide to us

  • Contact details — name, phone number, email address, and city, when you enquire through our website forms, WhatsApp, phone, or in person at our offices
  • Booking & identity documents — passport details, photographs, visa application information, date of birth, nationality, and travel companion details, as required by airlines, hotels, embassies and immigration authorities to process your booking
  • Pilgrimage information — for Hajj & Umrah packages, details required by Saudi authorities such as vaccination records and mahram relationships where applicable
  • Forex transaction details — information required under RBI regulations for currency exchange, including PAN and purpose of remittance where applicable
  • Newsletter subscription — your email address, if you opt in

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data when you browse our website, such as pages visited, device type and approximate location, via analytics (see Section 5)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage your bookings: flights, visas, hotels, tour packages, Hajj & Umrah, and forex
  • To communicate with you about your enquiry, booking status, itinerary changes and travel requirements
  • To comply with legal obligations, including immigration, aviation and RBI requirements
  • To send you offers and travel updates by email or WhatsApp, only where you have consented; you can opt out at any time
  • To improve our website and services

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

3. Who We Share It With

We share your information only as needed to deliver the services you request:

  • Airlines, hotels and transport providers to confirm your bookings
  • Embassies, consulates, visa processing centres and immigration authorities to process visa applications you ask us to handle
  • Saudi-authorised Umrah/Hajj service providers for pilgrimage arrangements
  • Regulators and government authorities where required by law
  • Service providers such as our website hosting and analytics providers, bound to use data only on our behalf

Where your booking involves travel outside India, the transfer of your details to overseas providers (for example, a hotel in Dubai or Saudi visa authorities) is a necessary part of performing your booking.

4. Payments

Payments are collected through bank transfer, UPI, or at our offices. We do not store your complete card numbers or banking passwords on our website. Any online payment is processed by the respective bank or payment provider under their own security standards.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies to keep the site functioning and to understand, in aggregate, how visitors use it (for example, which pages are most popular). Analytics data is used only in statistical form. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings; the site will continue to work with cookies disabled, though some features may be less convenient.

6. Data Security & Retention

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information — our website is served over HTTPS, and access to booking documents within our team is limited to staff who need them to process your travel. We retain booking records for as long as required for legal, tax and regulatory purposes, after which they are securely disposed of.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct information that is inaccurate or incomplete
  • Ask us to delete information we no longer need to retain
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11. We will respond within a reasonable time and in line with applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

We collect information about children (for example, for a child's flight ticket or visa) only when provided by a parent or legal guardian as part of a family booking, and we use it solely for that booking.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website and messages may contain links to third-party websites, such as airlines, embassies or social media platforms. Their privacy practices are their own; we encourage you to review their policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version, and significant changes will be highlighted on this page.
